Educational Disclaimer

NutriScope is an educational tool, not medical advice.

Calorie and nutrient values are approximations derived from standard food composition references and may vary based on brand, preparation method, portion size, and ingredient quality. BMR/TDEE estimates use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, which is a population-level formula and may not reflect individual metabolic variation.

Recommended Dietary Allowances (RDAs) shown are general adult guidelines and do not account for pregnancy, lactation, medical conditions, medications, or lab-confirmed deficiencies.

Always consult a registered dietitian, nutritionist, or physician before making significant dietary changes, starting supplementation, or addressing suspected nutrient deficiencies — especially Iron, Vitamin D, B12, Calcium, or Vitamin B6/Folate, which require blood testing for accurate assessment.

This tool does not store or transmit your data — all calculations happen locally in your browser.
